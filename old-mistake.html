Chapter 3 :- An old mistake.
In a realm where magic intertwines with time,
There lived a dark wizard, harboring secrets sublime.
With powers that twisted and turned through the night,
His heart filled with mischief, his intentions not quite right.

Yet deep within his core, a love did reside,
A wife, a Hollywood actress, forever by his side.
Their love like a tapestry, woven strong and wide,
Together, they dreamed of forever, side by side.

But alas, in pursuit of his dark, twisted ways,
The wizard made a mistake, lingering for countless days.
His conscience weighed heavy, his soul filled with remorse,
He knew he must rewind time, to rectify his course.

Leaving his wife and young son behind,
He embarked on a quest, a journey to rewind.
Back to a time where his mistake was made,
To rewrite history, to fix the life that had frayed.

Yet as he traveled through the portal, time torn asunder,
An evil sorcerer awaited, plotting a spell of thunder.
A wicked soccer, enchanted and vile,
Yearned to bind the dark wizard, force him to beguile.

In the past, as the wizard emerged,
The evil soccer cast its spell, now fully submerged.
Forced to commit unspeakable deeds,
Darkness clouded his mind, his soul in twisted weeds.

But deep within his memories, love's essence remained,
His wife and young son, their faces unrestrained.
Through the chaos and torment, their love burned bright,
Awakening his heart, guiding him to see the light.

With every step he took, a battle he waged,
Against the evil spell, as time continued to age.
He fought with his magic, and with every breath,
To break free from the sorcerer's hex.

In his darkest moments, love became his guide,
Guiding him toward redemption, casting evil aside.
With a resolve unyielding, he shattered the fetter,
Reclaiming his identity, his soul made whole and better.

Returning to the present, with lessons learned,
The dark wizard knew his wife he must yearn.
He vowed to make amends, to cherish every day,
Never to stray again, never to go astray.

He sought forgiveness from his wife's loving glow,
And their son, whose innocence started to grow.
Together, they rebuilt the love that was bruised,
A family bound by love that could never be diffused.

And so, the dark wizard transformed his ways,
Harnessing his powers to bring light through the gray.
Love triumphed over darkness, redemption was found,
In a magical realm where love's magic did abound.
